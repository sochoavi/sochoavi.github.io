Assistant Professor, Data Science

Avishek Majumder

I am an Assistant Professor and researcher working at the intersection of cryptography, databases, and data security. My research focuses on building secure, privacy-aware, and practical systems, with interests in searchable encryption, secure databases, post-quantum cryptography, and privacy-preserving query processing.

  • Krea University, Sri City
  • PhD in Computer Science, Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata
  • Former Assistant Professor, UPES Dehradun
